Categories

You’re almost certainly here because you clicked a link on a search engine results page. That link was taking you to one of my ‘category’ pages: a page that contained all the posts in that category e.g. http://blog.floehopper.org/articles/category/mocha_release. Like Chris Roos, I didn’t like these pages, because they’re basically search results within search results. So I’ve got rid of them.

In the process of removing these category pages, I’ve tagged all my posts in Delicious with the category, so that you can replicate the old ‘category’ pages. To do so, you’ll need to search my bookmarks for the jamesmead.org tag and whichever category it was that you were trying to visit e.g. to see all my posts in the mocha_release category, you can visit http://delicious.com/jamesmead/jamesmead.org+mocha_release (replace mocha_release with the category you were trying to find).
